What is Biomass?

BIOMASS (BIO-Medical Affiliated Stanford Students is a student council representing the graduate students of Stanford Medical School (SMS). BIOMASS's executive council is composed of 1-2 member(s) from each department &/or interdepartmental program. This council in turn elects three officers: a President, a Vice-President, and a Treasurer. BIOMASS is involved in organizing, funding, and sponsoring a variety of student activities including happy hours, ski trips, seminars and symposia. In addition BIOMASS provides student representation on the Council of Diversity, SMS Faculty Senate Meetings, and Women in Science and the Stanford Multicultural Scientists programs.
