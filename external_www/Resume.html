    • Research Assistant, Harvard University, May 1989 - December 1989
        Undergraduate research and summer employment in the lab of Prof. Fakhri A. Bazzaz. Helped design and conduct study on the effects of variation in light and water quality and quantity on the physiology of four species of birch (Betula). Also conducted independent experiment on circadian rhythms in Abutilon theophrasti.

        Internship with Prof. William A. Calder III, University of Arizona. Monitored weight and temperature rhythms, dive energetics, foraging strategies, nesting success, and behavior of alpine hummingbirds.
