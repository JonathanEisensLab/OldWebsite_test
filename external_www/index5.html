Jonathan A. Eisen's Home Page

   I am an Assistant Investigator in the Microbial Genomics Department at The Institute for Genomic Research in Rockville, Maryland. Before coming to TIGR, I was a Ph.D. student in Phil Hanawalt's laboratory in the Department of Biological Sciences at Stanford University. Most of the stuff that is on this web site relates to my research. Some miscellaneous personal information can be found here or at the bottom of the page.


Previous and Ongoing Research
